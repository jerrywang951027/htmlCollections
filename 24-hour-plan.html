Reliability becomes a component responsibility, not a prompt-only expectation.

Two-person execution

Component ownership controls the 24-hour schedule

The phases remain sequential at the contract level, but the two owners work in parallel inside each time block. H21–H24 is reserved for cross-component integration, adversarial QA, deployment freeze, and demo rehearsal.
